### Python - Numpy & Scipy
#### Numpy
- 数组对象 - `numpy.array()`
    - 数组创建 - `numpy.array()`, `numpy.zeros()`, `numpy.ones()`, `numpy.arange()`, `numpy.identity()`, `numpy.vstack()`, `numpy.hstack()`
    - 算术运算 - 与标量的运算 + - * /, 与数组的运算 - 元素乘 *, 矩阵乘 `numpy.dot()`
    - `numpy.sin()`, `numpy.cos()`, `numpy.exp()`, `numpy.log()`, `numpy.sqrt()`
    - 数组变形 - `reshape()`, `ravel()`, `transpose()`
