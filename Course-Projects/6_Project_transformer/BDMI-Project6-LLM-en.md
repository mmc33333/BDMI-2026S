## **Project Topic Guidelines**

### **Thematic Study (Mandatory)**  
Choose one of the following topics:

1. Select a paper on Transformer models and study aspects such as self-attention mechanisms, efficient implementations, and inference optimization.  
   - It is recommended to search for papers on arXiv, look for relevant repositories on GitHub/Gitee ({Transformer etc.}), or refer to [paperswithcode](https://paperswithcode.com/sota).  

2. Study a cutting-edge international paper on Chain of Thought (CoT) reasoning, including but not limited to:  
   - Reference key AI research institutions, such as:  
     - [Google AI Research](https://ai.google.com)  
     - [Facebook AI](https://ai.facebook.com/research)  
     - [OpenAI](https://openai.com)  
     - [Allen AI](https://allenai.org)  
     - [DeepSeek](https://github.com/deepseek-ai)  
     - [Zhipu AI](https://github.com/MetaGLM), etc.  
   - If uncertain, students may choose a topic provided by the instructor.  

#### **Assignment Requirements:**
- Submit both a WORD/PDF report and a PPT presentation.  
- The WORD/PDF report should be around 3000 words, but there is no strict length limit.  
- The PPT presentation should contain 8-15 slides.  
- Figures, images, and references must include proper citations.  

3. **Challenging Option (Compete in a Quantitative AI Challenge!)**  
   - Participate in the [AI Model Challenge](../7_Project_AI-Quant/AI模型邀请赛.md).

## **Grading Criteria**
- **Engagement (20%)**: Clearly explaining the problem and solution in an engaging way.
- **Feasibility (20%)**: It works.
- **Technical Difficulty (20%)**: Complexity and challenges in the solution.
- **Innovation (20%)**: Original contributions.
- **Demonstrability (20%)**: A working demo is preferred.
