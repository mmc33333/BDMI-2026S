Task: Train a small transformer model to generate Chinese news texts.  
Train Data: 19910 pieces of Chinese news texts (Provided by course team)
Test Data: 500 pieces of Chinese news texts (Provided by course team)
Evaluation Metrics:  
(1) The smaller the model parameter count, the better.  
(2) The shorter the runtime, the better.  
(3) Grammar correctness (max: 10, the higher the better).
(4) Content consistency (max: 10, the higher the better).
(5) Expression rigor (max: 10, the higher the better).
